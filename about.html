Company Overview

Toro Royal Mining Limited is a pioneering African mining company specializing in lithium processing and sustainable mineral extraction. With registration number RC: 8453730, we are at the forefront of the global transition to clean energy, providing essential materials for battery technology and renewable energy storage.

Our comprehensive operations span from raw material extraction to refined lithium processing and strategic shipping partnerships, positioning us as a key player in the electric vehicle revolution and sustainable energy future.
